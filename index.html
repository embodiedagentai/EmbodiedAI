Overview

Generalist Embodied Agent AI (GEAI) is a family of systems that generate effective actions in a given environment based on the understanding of multimodal sensory input. With the advent of large foundation models, numerous GEAI systems have been proposed in fields ranging from basic research to applications. While these research areas are growing rapidly by integrating with the traditional technologies of each domain, they share common interests such as data collection, benchmarking, and ethical perspectives. In this workshop, we focus on the some representative research areas of Embodied GEAI, namely embodied-multimodality, robotics, gaming (VR/AR/MR), and healthcare, etc., and we aim to provide comprehensive knowledge on the common concerns discussed in these fields. As a result we expect the participants to learn the fundamentals of GEAI and gain insights to further advance their research. Specific learning outcomes include:

  • GEAI Overview: A deep dive into its principles and roles in contemporary applications, providing attendees with a thorough grasp of its importance and uses.
  • Methodologies: Detailed examples of how large foundation model enhance GEAIs, illustrated through case studies in embodied virtual and real world, e.g., robotics, gaming, and healthcare with human feedback.
  • Performance Evaluation: Guidance on the assessment of GEAIs with relevant datasets, focusing on their effectiveness and generalization.
  • Ethical Considerations: A discussion on the societal impacts and ethical challenges of deploying Embodied Agent AI, highlighting responsible development practices.
  • Emerging Trends and Future Challenges: Categorize the latest developments in each domain and discuss the future directions.

Led by esteemed experts from academia and industry, we expect that the workshop will be an interactive and enriching experience, complete with lectures, case studies, and Q&A sessions ensuring a comprehensive and engaging learning experience for all participants.
